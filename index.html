About Our Church

Welcome to the Saint Michael and Saint Yared Tigray Orthodox Tewahedo Church Baltimore. We are a community dedicated to teaching and preaching Christianity, following the rich traditions of the Tigray Orthodox Tewahedo faith.

Mission

Our mission is to share the Gospel of Christ, foster spiritual growth, and serve our community in the love of God. We seek to build a welcoming environment where faith, fellowship, and worship bring us closer to God and each other.
